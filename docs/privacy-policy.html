Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2024

Introduction

Five Crowns ("we", "our", or "the app") is a multiplayer card game application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address - Used for account creation, login, and password recovery
  • Username - Your chosen display name visible to other players
  • Display name - Optional name shown during games

Game Data

  • Game history and scores
  • Friends list
  • Game preferences and settings

Camera and Microphone

The app requests access to your device's camera and microphone to enable live video and voice chat during multiplayer games. This feature is optional and can be disabled at any time.

  • Camera - Used for live video during games (optional)
  • Microphone - Used for voice chat during games (on by default, can be muted)

Important: Video and audio streams are transmitted in real-time to other players in your game session only. We do not record, store, or have access to your video or audio content.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To enable multiplayer gameplay with friends
  • To display leaderboards and game statistics
  • To send account-related emails (verification, password reset)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • JWT-based authentication

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Your username and display name are visible to other players
  • Video/audio is streamed to players in your current game session
  • When required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Disable camera and microphone access at any time

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
